Who we are

Connected Gainesville is a community initiative to bring affordable, high-speed, broadband internet to everyone in our community.

“The internet is becoming the town square of the global village of tomorrow.”.

Bill Gates

The Issue

Our community lags behind the rest of the state in internet affordability and connectivity.

Internet is and will continue to be a necessity in the 21st century

Internet as a necessity

Some examples:

Students need it for homework

Job seekers need it to search

Businesses need it to connect with customers

Our goal

Enable the best local solutions to solve these problems

Bring affordable Broadband to everyone

Impact to the city

Promote equal access to education and healthcare

Bring new jobs

Keep our best from leaving

What are we asking for?

What are we asking for?

A study to find how we can provide:

• Affordable high-speed internet for everyone in our community• Low cost options for working poor• Next generation connectivity for our businesses

What are we asking for?

The study should:

• Review all options for better connectivity.• Analyze risks, costs, revenue, feasibility, and impact to the community.• Work with the county, University of Florida, and local municipalities to find a holistic solution.• Leverage 21st century utility initiative, smart city upgrades, and public service communication updgrades.
